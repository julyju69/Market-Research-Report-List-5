<p><p>Pouch films are versatile packaging materials categorized mainly by their composition. Aluminum foil offers strong barrier properties, ideal for food preservation. CPP film is favored for its sealability and moisture resistance, and LLDPE film is used for its flexibility and toughness. Other materials include various blends and specialty films, catering to specific market needs.</p></p>
