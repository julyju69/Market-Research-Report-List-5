<p><p>Bone tumor ablation devices encompass various techniques used to treat bone tumors. Radiofrequency ablation employs heat generated by radio waves to destroy tumor cells, while microwave ablation uses electromagnetic energy to achieve similar results through higher temperatures. Cryoablation involves freezing tumor tissue to induce cell death. Other methods may include laser ablation and ultrasound-guided techniques. Each approach offers unique advantages in terms of precision, recovery time, and minimally invasive options, catering to diverse clinical needs in oncology.</p></p>
