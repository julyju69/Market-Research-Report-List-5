<p><p>An Uninterruptible Power System (UPS) is a device that provides backup power to critical electrical devices in the event of a power failure, ensuring continuous operation and preventing data loss. Industries are increasingly investing in UPS systems to enhance operational reliability, particularly in sectors like IT and finance. There is also a growing interest in modular and scalable UPS systems that can be customized to meet specific energy needs. Furthermore, the integration of smart technologies for monitoring and optimizing UPS performance is gaining traction. Overall, the Uninterruptible Power System (UPS) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.40% during the forecast period.</p></p>
