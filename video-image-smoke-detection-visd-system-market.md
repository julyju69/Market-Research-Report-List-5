<p><p>The Video Image Smoke Detection (VISD) system market is divided into active and passive detection systems. Active detection systems continuously monitor video feeds, analyzing images for smoke patterns, providing prompt alerts to prevent fire escalation. In contrast, passive detection systems rely on predefined criteria or algorithms to assess video data periodically, triggering alarms based on established smoke indicators. Both systems aim to enhance fire safety and response efficiency, serving diverse applications in commercial and industrial sectors.</p></p>
