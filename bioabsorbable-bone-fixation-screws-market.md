<p><p>Bioabsorbable bone fixation screws are medical devices designed to stabilize fractured bones while gradually dissolving in the body over time, eliminating the need for a second surgery to remove metallic hardware. These screws are primarily made from biocompatible materials such as polylactic acid (PLA) or polyglycolic acid (PGA), which break down into harmless byproducts that can be absorbed by the body. The increasing prevalence of orthopedic surgeries, including sports injuries and trauma cases, is driving the demand for these innovative fixation devices.</p><p>The Bioabsorbable Bone Fixation Screws Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period, attributed to advancements in material science and surgical techniques.
