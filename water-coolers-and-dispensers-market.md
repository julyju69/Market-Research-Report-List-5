<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Cooler (BWC)</li><li>Point-of-Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Water Coolers and Dispensers market is primarily divided into two types: Bottled Water Coolers (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) dispensers. BWC systems use large bottles of water that are placed on top of the cooler, making them portable and easy to replace. In contrast, POU dispensers connect directly to a water supply, providing instant access to filtered water and eliminating the need for bottle replacements. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
