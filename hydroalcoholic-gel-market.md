<p><p>The hydroalcoholic gel market is primarily categorized into two types: ethyl alcohol-based gels and isopropyl alcohol-based gels. Ethyl type gels typically contain ethanol, known for its effectiveness in killing germs and viruses, making it popular for personal and professional use. Isopropyl alcohol type gels utilize isopropanol, valued for its rapid evaporation and stronger antimicrobial properties. Both types are extensively used in healthcare, personal care, and industrial sectors to ensure hygiene and reduce infection risk.</p></p>
