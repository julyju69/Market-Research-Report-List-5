<p><p>The auto dash camera market is primarily categorized into two types: single-channel and multi-channel dash cameras. Single-channel auto dash cameras capture video from a single lens, typically focusing on the road ahead. In contrast, multi-channel auto dash cameras are equipped with multiple lenses, allowing for comprehensive coverage by recording various angles, including the rear and interior of the vehicle. This differentiation caters to varying consumer needs for safety, surveillance, and evidence collection while driving.</p></p>
