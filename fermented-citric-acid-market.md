<p><strong>The Fermented Citric Acid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Citric Acid Anhydrous(CAA)</li><li>Citric Acid Monohydrate(CAM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fermented citric acid market includes two primary types: Citric Acid Anhydrous (CAA) and Citric Acid Monohydrate (CAM). CAA is produced without water, resulting in a more concentrated form, useful in industries requiring lower moisture content, such as food preservation and pharmaceutical applications. CAM, containing one molecule of water, is commonly used in food and beverage production for its acidity and flavor enhancement. Both forms are derived from microbial fermentation, reflecting the growing demand for natural additives in various sectors.</p></p>
