<p><p>The dental X-ray sensors market is primarily segmented into intraoral and extraoral types. Intraoral X-rays involve sensors placed inside the mouth to capture detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding structures, making them essential for diagnosing cavities, root issues, and bone health. Extraoral X-rays, on the other hand, involve sensors outside the mouth to capture broader images, such as panoramic views, which help in assessing overall dental structure and planning treatments like orthodontics or implants.</p></p>
