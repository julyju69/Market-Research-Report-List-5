<p><p>The electronic cigarette juice market primarily consists of three types: PG base, VG base, and blends of both. PG (propylene glycol) base provides a throat hit similar to traditional tobacco, enhances flavor, and is thinner, making it easier to vaporize. VG (vegetable glycerin) base offers a sweeter taste, thicker consistency, and produces denser vapor clouds, appealing to cloud chasers. Blended PG and VG juices aim to combine the benefits of both, catering to diverse preferences among vapers for flavor and vapor production.</p></p>
