<p><ul><li>SPD</li><li>PDLC</li><li>Electrochromic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dynamic glass market comprises three primary types: Suspended Particle Device (SPD), Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC), and Electrochromic glass. SPD offers rapid tint control by adjusting the alignment of nanoparticles, allowing customization of transparency. PDLC technology utilizes liquid crystals that switch between opaque and transparent states, enhancing privacy. Electrochromic glass changes its color and opacity in response to electric voltage, providing energy efficiency and glare reduction. Together, these technologies enable innovative solutions for architectural and automotive applications.</p></p>
