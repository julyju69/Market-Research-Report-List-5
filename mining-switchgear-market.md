<p><ul><li>Fixed Installation</li><li>Portable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mining switchgear market is categorized into fixed installation and portable types. Fixed installation switchgear is permanently installed in mining operations, offering robust protection and control for electrical systems in hazardous environments. It is typically used in large-scale operations for reliable, long-term performance. In contrast, portable switchgear is designed for flexibility and mobility, allowing it to be easily relocated between different mining sites. This type is ideal for smaller operations or temporary projects where adaptability and quick deployment are essential.</p></p>
