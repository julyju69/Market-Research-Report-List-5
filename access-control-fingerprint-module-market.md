<p><p>The Access Control Fingerprint Module market can be categorized into two primary types: capacitive fingerprint modules and optical fingerprint modules. Capacitive fingerprint modules utilize small capacitor arrays to capture fingerprint patterns, offering enhanced accuracy and durability against wear. In contrast, optical fingerprint modules employ light to capture an image of the fingerprint, often providing higher visibility in various lighting conditions. Both technologies are essential for secure access control systems, catering to diverse applications in security and identification.</p></p>
