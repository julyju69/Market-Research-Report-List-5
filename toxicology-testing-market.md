<p><p>The toxicology testing market encompasses several advanced technologies. Cell culture technology involves using living cells to study toxic effects, enabling more accurate biological responses. High throughput technologies accelerate testing processes, allowing for the rapid assessment of numerous compounds. Molecular imaging technologies visualize biological processes at the molecular level, enhancing understanding of toxicity mechanisms. Lastly, omics technologies, such as genomics and proteomics, analyze comprehensive biological data, helping to identify potential toxic effects at a systemic level. Each technology plays a crucial role in improving safety assessments in various industries.</p></p>
