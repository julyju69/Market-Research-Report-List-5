<p><p>The vehicle crash test system market encompasses two primary types: passive safety testing and active safety testing. Passive safety testing evaluates the vehicle's ability to protect occupants during a collision through structural integrity and restraint systems, such as airbags and seatbelts. In contrast, active safety testing focuses on systems designed to prevent accidents, such as advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), collision avoidance technologies, and vehicle stability controls. Together, these testing methods enhance vehicle safety and performance in real-world scenarios.</p></p>
