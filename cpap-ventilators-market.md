<p><strong>The CPAP Ventilators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-CPAP</li><li>Auto CPAP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CPAP ventilators market primarily features two types: Fixed-CPAP and Auto CPAP. Fixed-CPAP delivers a constant air pressure to keep the airway open during sleep, typically set by a healthcare provider. In contrast, Auto CPAP, also known as APAP, adjusts the pressure automatically based on real-time airway resistance, providing a variable airflow tailored to the user’s needs. This adaptability can enhance comfort and efficacy, appealing to a broader range of patients with varying sleep apnea symptoms.</p></p>
