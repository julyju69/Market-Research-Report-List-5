<p><p>Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) services encompass two primary market types: international long-distance VoIP calls and domestic VoIP calls. International long-distance VoIP calls allow users to make calls across countries using internet technology, reducing costs significantly compared to traditional phone services. Domestic VoIP calls, on the other hand, enable users to make calls within the same country over the internet, providing a cost-effective solution for both personal and business communication. Both services enhance connectivity and reduce expenses in diverse communication environments.</p></p>
