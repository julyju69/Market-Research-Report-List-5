<p><p>Handheld optical attenuators are devices used to reduce optical signal strength for testing and troubleshooting fiber optic networks. The single-mode market focuses on attenuators designed for single-mode fibers, which transmit light directly down the fiber's core, suitable for long distances. In contrast, the multimode market addresses attenuators for multimode fibers, where light travels through multiple paths, making them ideal for shorter distances. Both types cater to different network applications and performance requirements, ensuring accurate signal measurement and management.</p></p>
