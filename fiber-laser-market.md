<p><strong>The Fiber Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser is a type of fiber laser that produces a continuous beam of laser light, making it ideal for applications requiring steady and consistent laser power. Pulsed Fiber Laser, on the other hand, emits laser pulses in short bursts, providing precise control over the laser output for applications such as laser marking, engraving, and micromachining. Both types of fiber lasers are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, medical, and electronics for their high efficiency, reliability, and versatility.</p></p>
