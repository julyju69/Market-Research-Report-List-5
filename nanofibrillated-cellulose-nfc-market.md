<p><p>Nanofibrillated Cellulose (NFC) market encompasses three main types: Nanofibrillated Cellulose, Nanocrystalline Cellulose, and Bacterial Nanocellulose. Nanofibrillated Cellulose is a versatile material derived from wood or other cellulose sources, known for its high strength and lightweight properties. Nanocrystalline Cellulose consists of crystalline regions extracted from cellulose, offering superior mechanical and thermal properties. Bacterial Nanocellulose is produced by microorganisms and is praised for its purity and unique structural characteristics, making it valuable in biomedical and food applications.</p></p>
