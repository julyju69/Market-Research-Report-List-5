<p><p>The Household Non-invasive Ventilator market encompasses three primary types: CPAP, BiPAP, and APAP. CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) provides a steady airflow to keep airways open, ideal for obstructive sleep apnea. BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) offers two pressure settings for inhalation and exhalation, benefiting individuals with varying respiratory needs. APAP (Automatic Positive Airway Pressure) automatically adjusts pressure based on real-time breathing patterns, enhancing comfort and effectiveness. Each type caters to diverse respiratory conditions, enhancing patient care at home.</p></p>
