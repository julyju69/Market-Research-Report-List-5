<p><p>The Personal Emergency Response System (PERS) market encompasses three main types: Landline, Mobile, and Standalone systems. Standalone systems often feature integrated alert buttons and monitoring without reliance on external services, offering a self-contained solution for users seeking immediate assistance.</p></p>
