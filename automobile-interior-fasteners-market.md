<p><p>The Automobile Interior Fasteners Market is divided into two main types: threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners include screws and bolts, which allow for secure, adjustable connections between interior components like seats and dashboards. Non-threaded fasteners encompass clips, rivets, and snap fittings that provide a simpler assembly process without the need for threads. Both types are essential for ensuring durability, ease of assembly, and efficient manufacturing processes in vehicle interior design and production.</p></p>
