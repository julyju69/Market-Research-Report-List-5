<p><p>The wearable cardiac device market includes various technologies designed for monitoring heart health. Defibrillators are life-saving devices that deliver electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythms during cardiac arrest. Patch monitors, often adhesive and worn on the skin, continuously track heart activity, providing real-time data to both patients and healthcare providers. Holter monitors, portable electrocardiograms worn over a 24 to 48-hour period, record heart rhythms for detailed analysis, helping identify irregularities and guiding treatment decisions. Each type plays a crucial role in cardiac care and preventive health.</p></p>
