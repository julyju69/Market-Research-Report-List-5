<p><p>The natural cosmetics preservatives market encompasses various types, including lipids, acids, alcohols, and other natural compounds. Lipids, such as oils and fats, offer moisturizing properties while acting as barriers against microbial growth. Acids, like citric and sorbic acid, help maintain acidity levels and prevent spoilage. Alcohols, including ethanol and benzyl alcohol, possess antifungal and antibacterial properties, prolonging shelf life. Other natural preservatives may include extracts from plants and herbs, contributing additional benefits while ensuring product safety and stability.</p></p>
