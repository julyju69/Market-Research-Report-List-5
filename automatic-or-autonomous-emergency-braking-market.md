<p><ul><li>Forward Collision Warning</li><li>Dynamic Brake Support</li><li>Crash Imminent Braking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic or Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) market includes systems designed to prevent collisions. **Forward Collision Warning (FCW)** alerts drivers of potential front-end collisions. **Dynamic Brake Support (DBS)** enhances braking force when the driver applies insufficient pressure. **Crash Imminent Braking (CIB)** automatically applies brakes to avoid or mitigate an imminent crash. These technologies work together to improve road safety by detecting hazards, assisting drivers, and taking autonomous action when necessary, reducing accidents and enhancing vehicle safety.</p></p>
