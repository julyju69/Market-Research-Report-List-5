<p><ul><li>Vertical Polarization</li><li>Circular Polarization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS antenna market is characterized by two main types: vertical polarization and circular polarization. Vertical polarization antennas are designed to receive signals transmitted vertically, making them ideal for applications with fixed or stationary setups, such as vehicles or base stations. In contrast, circular polarization antennas can receive signals from any orientation, enhancing performance in dynamic environments where signal direction varies, such as in mobile devices or drones. The choice between the two types depends on specific application requirements and operational conditions.</p></p>
