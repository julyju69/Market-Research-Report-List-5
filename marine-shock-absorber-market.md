<p><p>The Marine Shock Absorber market is primarily categorized into elastomer and plastic types. In contrast, plastic shock absorbers are lighter and resistant to corrosion, providing cost-effective solutions with moderate damping properties. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vessel performance and safety, ensuring a smoother ride and better protection against the stresses encountered at sea.</p></p>
