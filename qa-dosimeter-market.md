<p><p>The QA dosimeter market comprises various types, primarily including Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLDs), Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL) dosimeters, and other innovative devices. TLDs measure ionizing radiation exposure by capturing energy in a crystal and releasing light upon heating. OSL dosimeters utilize light to stimulate trapped electrons, allowing for precise dose measurements. Additionally, other dosimeters may include electronic devices or film-based technologies. Collectively, these products are essential for radiation monitoring in medical, industrial, and research applications.</p></p>
