<p><p>The LPG composite cylinders market is categorized into metal composite and non-metal composite types. Metal composite cylinders combine a metal liner with composite materials, offering durability and strength while reducing weight, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, non-metal composite cylinders are made entirely from lightweight composite materials, providing better corrosion resistance and portability. Both types cater to various consumer needs, enhancing safety and efficiency in LPG storage and transportation, especially in residential and commercial sectors.</p></p>
