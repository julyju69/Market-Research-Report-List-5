<p><p>Biopharmaceutical logistics services encompass two primary market types: cold chain logistics and non-cold chain logistics. Cold chain logistics involve the transportation and storage of temperature-sensitive biopharmaceuticals, such as vaccines and biologics, requiring strict temperature controls to maintain efficacy. In contrast, non-cold chain logistics pertain to biopharmaceuticals that do not require refrigeration, allowing for more flexible handling and storage conditions. Both types are crucial in ensuring the safe and efficient delivery of biopharmaceutical products to healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
