<p><ul><li>Low-Pressure Turbine</li><li>High-Pressure Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial jet engine market is segmented into low-pressure and high-pressure turbine types. High-pressure turbines are critical for compressing air within the engine, enhancing performance and fuel efficiency. They operate at higher temperatures and pressures, contributing to overall thrust. Low-pressure turbines, on the other hand, extract energy from exhaust gases to drive the fan and other components, optimizing efficiency and reducing noise. Together, these turbines are essential for the performance, efficiency, and environmental compliance of modern jet engines.</p></p>
