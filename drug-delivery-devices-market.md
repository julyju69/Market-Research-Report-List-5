<p><strong>The Drug Delivery Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection type</li><li>Mucosal type</li><li>Implanted type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drug delivery devices market comprises three main types: injection type, mucosal type, and implanted type. Injection type devices, such as syringes and auto-injectors, facilitate direct administration of medications into the body, ensuring rapid absorption. Mucosal type devices include nasal sprays and sublingual tablets, which deliver drugs through mucous membranes for quick systemic effects. Implanted type devices, like infusion pumps and biodegradable implants, provide sustained drug release over time, enhancing therapeutic efficacy while minimizing side effects.</p></p>
