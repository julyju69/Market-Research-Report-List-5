<p><ul><li>Emergency Breakaway Coupler</li><li>Pressure Coupler</li><li>Hydrant Coupler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace couplers market comprises various types, including emergency breakaway couplers, pressure couplers, and hydrant couplers. Emergency breakaway couplers are designed to safely disconnect fuel lines during unexpected movements, preventing spills and ensuring safety. Pressure couplers facilitate the efficient transfer of fuels under pressure, essential for aircraft refueling operations. Hydrant couplers connect fuel hydrants to refueling vehicles, allowing for quick and reliable fueling operations at airports. Together, these couplers enhance operational efficiency and safety in aerospace fueling systems.</p></p>
