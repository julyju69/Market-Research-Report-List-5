<p><ul><li>Roller Peristaltic Pump</li><li>Shoe type Peristaltic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peristaltic pumps are essential in the pharmaceutical industry for precise fluid transfer and dosing. Roller peristaltic pumps utilize rotating rollers to compress a flexible tube, pushing fluids efficiently. They are ideal for sterile applications due to minimal risk of contamination. Shoe-type peristaltic pumps, on the other hand, employ a shoe that glides along the tubing to create a peristaltic movement, offering gentle handling of delicate fluids. Both types ensure accurate flow rates, making them crucial in pharmaceutical manufacturing and research.</p></p>
