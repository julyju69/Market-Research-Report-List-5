<p><p>Coconut biodiesel is a renewable fuel derived from coconut oil, offering a sustainable alternative to conventional fossil fuels. It is produced through a process called transesterification, where triglycerides in coconut oil are converted into fatty acid methyl esters (FAME), which can be used as biodiesel. The demand for coconut biodiesel is driven by the growing need for eco-friendly fuel options and increased awareness of environmental issues.</p><p>The Coconut Biodiesel Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.4% during the forecast period, fueled by factors such as rising global energy demand, favorable government policies promoting renewable energy, and advancements in biodiesel production technologies.
