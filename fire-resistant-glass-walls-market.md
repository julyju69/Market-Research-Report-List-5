<p><p>The Fire-Resistant Glass Walls market is categorized into two main types: single-layer and double-layer fire-resistant glass walls. Single-layer fire-resistant glass walls consist of a single pane designed to withstand high temperatures, offering basic protection. In contrast, double-layer fire-resistant glass walls feature two panes, often with an air gap or insulating layer, providing enhanced thermal insulation and superior fire resistance. These products are essential for ensuring safety in commercial and residential buildings, maintaining aesthetics while meeting fire safety regulations.</p></p>
