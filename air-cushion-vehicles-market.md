<p><p>The air-cushion vehicles market is categorized into two main types: skirted air-cushion vehicles and sidewall air-cushion vehicles. Skirted air-cushion vehicles utilize flexible skirts to trap air beneath the vehicle, creating a cushion that enables smooth movement over various surfaces, ideal for transportation and rescue operations. In contrast, sidewall air-cushion vehicles feature rigid sidewalls, providing enhanced stability and support while also generating lift. Both types serve diverse applications in sectors like military, tourism, and maritime environments, emphasizing mobility and efficiency.</p></p>
