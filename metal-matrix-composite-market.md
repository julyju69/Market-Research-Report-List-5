<p><p>Metal Matrix Composite (MMC) is a type of material made by reinforcing a metal matrix with a secondary material, such as ceramics or fibers. Two common types of MMCs are Aluminum Metal Matrix Composite and Magnesium Metal Matrix Composite. Aluminum MMCs are known for their high strength and stiffness, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Magnesium MMCs offer lightweight properties and excellent thermal conductivity, making them suitable for electronic packaging and heat sink applications. Both types of MMCs offer superior performance compared to traditional metals.</p></p>
