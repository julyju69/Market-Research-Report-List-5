<p><strong>The Engineered Hardwood Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tongue-and-groove (TandG) Technology</li><li>Click-locking Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineered hardwood flooring features two primary installation technologies: tongue-and-groove (T&G) and click-locking. T&G technology involves planks with interlocking edges, allowing them to fit snugly together, which promotes stability and a seamless appearance. This traditional method often requires glue or nails for installation. In contrast, click-locking technology enables planks to snap together without the need for adhesives, allowing for a quicker and more user-friendly installation process. Both types offer durability and aesthetic appeal, catering to different consumer preferences and installation needs.</p></p>
