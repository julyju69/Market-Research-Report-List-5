Polytetrafluoroethylene excels in chemical resistance, while ceramic membranes offer high thermal stability and robustness. Metal membranes are utilized for extreme conditions. Molecular sieve composite membranes are ideal for selective separation, and glass membranes provide precise filtration capabilities in critical applications, enhancing performance across diverse industries.</p></p>
