<p><p>The parking meter device market consists of two primary types: single-space parking meters and multi-space parking kiosks. Single-space meters are designed for individual parking spots, allowing users to pay for time in a straightforward manner, often with cash or cards. Multi-space kiosks, on the other hand, serve multiple parking spaces at once, enabling users to pay at a central location and often incorporating advanced features like electronic payment options and ticket issuance. Both types enhance parking efficiency and revenue collection.</p></p>
