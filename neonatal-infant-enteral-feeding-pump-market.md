<p><p>The Neonatal Infant Enteral Feeding Pump market is primarily divided into two types: Continuous Feeding Type and Cycled Feeding Type. Continuous Feeding Pumps deliver a constant flow of nutrition to infants, ensuring a steady intake, which is beneficial for those with specific medical needs. In contrast, Cycled Feeding Pumps provide nutrition in intervals, mimicking natural feeding patterns, allowing for digestion breaks. Both types cater to different clinical requirements and preferences, optimizing enteral feeding for neonates and ensuring their growth and development.</p></p>
