<p><strong>The Allergy Immunotherapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subcutaneous Immunotherapy</li><li>Sublingual Immunotherapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Allergy immunotherapy is a treatment aimed at desensitizing the immune system to allergens. Subcutaneous immunotherapy (SCIT) involves administering allergen extracts via injections, typically in a clinic, offering long-term relief for various allergies. Sublingual immunotherapy (SLIT), on the other hand, involves placing allergen tablets or drops under the tongue, allowing for at-home administration. Both methods aim to reduce allergic symptoms and improve quality of life, yet they differ in delivery methods, administration frequency, and patient comfort.</p></p>
