<p><p>The organic carrot flour market consists primarily of two types: spray drying carrot powder and freeze-dried carrot powder. Spray drying involves rapidly evaporating moisture from carrot puree, resulting in a fine powder with concentrated nutrients and flavor, ideal for bulk applications. In contrast, freeze-dried carrot powder preserves the carrots' cell structure through low-temperature processing, retaining a greater amount of vitamins and minerals, making it suitable for high-quality, health-focused products. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
