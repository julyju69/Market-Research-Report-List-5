<p><strong>The C-Peptide Test Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li><li>Time-resolved Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The C-Peptide Test Kit market is categorized into two main types: Chemiluminescence Immunoassay and Time-resolved Immunoassay. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay leverages light emission to quantify C-peptide levels, offering high sensitivity and rapid results. In contrast, Time-resolved Immunoassay utilizes time-resolved fluorescence to measure C-peptide concentrations, providing improved specificity and minimal background interference. Both methods are essential for assessing insulin production in diabetes management, with varying applications in clinical diagnostics and research settings.</p></p>
