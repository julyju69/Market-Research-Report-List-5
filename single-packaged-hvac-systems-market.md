<p><p>Single packaged HVAC systems are typically classified into two main types: air-cooled systems and water-cooled systems. Air-cooled systems utilize outdoor air to dissipate heat through a condenser coil, making them suitable for areas with moderate climates. On the other hand, water-cooled systems use water as a heat transfer medium, providing efficient cooling in high-temperature environments. Both types of systems offer convenient all-in-one solutions for heating, cooling, and ventilation, making them popular choices for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
