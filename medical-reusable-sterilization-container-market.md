<p><p>The medical reusable sterilization container market is primarily segmented into two types: filter type and valve type. Filter type containers incorporate built-in filtration systems that allow for air circulation during sterilization, enhancing the efficiency and maintaining sterility. Valve type containers utilize one-way valves to manage airflow, preventing contamination while facilitating the sterilization process. Both types are designed to meet stringent regulatory standards, offering healthcare facilities safe and durable solutions for sterilizing and transporting medical instruments and devices.</p></p>
