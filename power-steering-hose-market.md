<p><p>The Power Steering Hose market consists of High Pressure and Low Pressure Power Steering Hoses. High Pressure Power Steering Hoses are designed to withstand the intense hydraulic pressure generated in power steering systems, ensuring efficient fluid transfer and responsiveness. In contrast, Low Pressure Power Steering Hoses handle return flows and are subjected to less pressure, focusing on flexibility and ease of installation. Both types are essential for the smooth operation of power steering mechanisms in vehicles, enhancing drivability and safety.</p></p>
