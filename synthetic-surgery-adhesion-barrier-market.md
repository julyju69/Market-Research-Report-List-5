<p><p>Synthetic Surgery Adhesion Barriers are medical products designed to prevent adhesions, which are fibrous bands of tissue that can form after surgery. These barriers are typically made from biocompatible materials and are strategically placed during surgical procedures to separate tissues and reduce the risk of post-operative complications. The primary applications include abdominal, pelvic, and orthopedic surgeries.</p><p>The market for Synthetic Surgery Adhesion Barriers is witnessing significant growth, driven by an increase in surgical procedures worldwide and a rising awareness of post-surgical complications.
