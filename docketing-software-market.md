<p><p>Docketing software can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premises markets. Cloud-based docketing solutions are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, easy access from anywhere, automatic updates, and reduced IT maintenance. They are ideal for firms seeking scalability and remote collaboration. On-premises docketing software is installed locally on a firm’s servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. This option is preferred by organizations with strict compliance requirements or those that need high-performance processing without relying on internet connectivity.</p></p>
