<p><strong>The Pressure Controller for Semiconductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Controller</li><li>Gauge Pressure Controller</li><li>Differential Pressure Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure controllers in the semiconductor market regulate pressure levels to ensure optimal manufacturing conditions. Absolute pressure controllers measure pressure relative to a vacuum, providing precise control for sensitive processes. Gauge pressure controllers measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, commonly used in general applications. Differential pressure controllers monitor the pressure difference between two points, crucial for processes like filtration and flow management. Each type plays a vital role in maintaining equipment efficiency and product quality in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
