<p><p>The Steerable Pod Propulsor market consists of three main types: Fixed Blade, Folding Blade, and No Blade. Fixed Blade propulsors feature rigid blades designed for efficient thrust and maneuverability. Folding Blade systems allow blades to retract or fold, optimizing drag and efficiency during non-operational phases. No Blade designs eliminate traditional blades entirely, utilizing other propulsion technologies for streamlined performance and reduced maintenance. Each type offers distinct advantages suited to different maritime and naval applications, enhancing overall vessel performance.</p></p>
