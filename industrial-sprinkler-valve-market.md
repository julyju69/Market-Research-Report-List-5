<p><ul><li>Linear Spray Valve</li><li>Radial Spray Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial sprinkler valve market features two primary types: linear spray valves and radial spray valves. Linear spray valves deliver a concentrated stream of water over a set path, making them ideal for targeted suppression. In contrast, radial spray valves disperse water in a wider circular pattern, effectively covering larger areas. Both valve types play crucial roles in fire protection systems, with specific applications suited to different environments, ensuring optimal fire safety and control in industrial settings.</p></p>
