<p><p>The H. pylori test kit market includes two main types: antibody tests and antigen tests. Antibody tests detect the presence of antibodies to H. pylori in the blood, indicating past or current infection. Antigen tests, on the other hand, identify H. pylori proteins in stool samples, providing a direct assessment of active infection. Both test types play critical roles in diagnosing and managing H. pylori-related conditions, helping healthcare professionals make informed treatment decisions based on infection status.</p></p>
