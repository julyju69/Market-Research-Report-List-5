Flat Belt Rotocure Machines utilize a flat belt system for efficient rubber curing, offering consistent temperature control and uniform curing pressure. In contrast, Triangle Belt Rotocure Machines use triangular belts, enhancing power transmission and providing improved durability under high-speed operations. Both types cater to the rubber manufacturing industry, but differ in design and efficiency, impacting their applications based on production needs.</p></p>
