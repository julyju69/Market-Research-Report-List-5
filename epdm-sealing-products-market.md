<p><p>The EPDM sealing products market can be categorized into two main types: sulfonation and thermoplasticity. Sulfonation refers to the process of chemically modifying EPDM rubber to enhance its properties, resulting in improved adhesion and durability for sealing applications. In contrast, thermoplastic EPDM involves the incorporation of thermoplastic materials, allowing for increased flexibility and ease of processing. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics, where reliable sealing solutions are essential for performance and longevity.</p></p>
