<p><p>The systemic herbicide market is divided into two main types: selective and non-selective herbicides. Selective herbicides target specific weeds without harming desired plants, making them ideal for crops and gardens. Non-selective herbicides, on the other hand, kill all vegetation they come into contact with, often used for land-clearing or managing unwanted plants in non-crop areas. Both types play critical roles in agriculture and landscaping, providing essential solutions for weed management while influencing market dynamics based on application needs.</p></p>
