<p><strong>The Refractories Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaped Refractories</li><li>Unshaped Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractories market is divided into two primary types: shaped and unshaped refractories. Shaped refractories are pre-formed products, such as bricks and tiles, designed for specific applications and offer high stability and durability. Conversely, unshaped refractories, also known as monolithic refractories, are used in a plastic or flowable form, making them suitable for complex shapes and repair jobs. Both types are crucial in high-temperature environments, such as steel, cement, and glass industries, enhancing operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
