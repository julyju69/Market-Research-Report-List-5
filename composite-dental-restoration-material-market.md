<p><strong>The Composite Dental Restoration Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin-based Composites</li><li>Poly-acid Modified Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite dental restoration material market comprises primarily of resin-based composites and poly-acid modified composites. Resin-based composites are widely used for their aesthetic appeal and superior mechanical properties, making them ideal for anterior restorations. Poly-acid modified composites, also known as glass ionomer-based materials, release fluoride and bond chemically to tooth structures, providing a supportive role in cavities and pediatric dentistry. Both types serve distinct purposes, catering to different clinical needs and patient preferences in dental restoration.</p></p>
