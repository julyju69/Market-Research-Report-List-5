<p><strong>The Threadlocker Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Strength Level</li><li>Medium Strength Level</li><li>High Strength Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threadlocker fluid is categorized into three strength levels: low, medium, and high. Low-strength threadlockers are ideal for small fasteners that require frequent adjustments, easily allowing disassembly. Medium-strength variations offer a balance, providing resistance without making parts permanently secure, suitable for general applications. High-strength threadlockers are designed for heavy-duty applications, securing large fasteners against vibration and extreme conditions, but they often necessitate heat or special tools for removal. Each type serves specific needs in mechanical maintenance and assembly.</p></p>
