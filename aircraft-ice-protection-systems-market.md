<p><strong>The Aircraft Ice Protection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic De-icing Boots</li><li>Electro-thermal</li><li>Electro-mechanical</li><li>TKS Ice Protection</li><li>Bleed air</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Ice Protection Systems market includes various technologies to ensure safe flight in icy conditions. Pneumatic de-icing boots use inflatable rubber surfaces to remove ice. Electro-thermal systems apply heat directly to critical surfaces to melt ice. Electro-mechanical systems utilize motors to activate de-icing mechanisms. TKS ice protection employs a fluid that prevents ice formation. Bleed air systems utilize hot air from the engines to maintain surface temperatures. Passive systems rely on surface design to minimize ice accumulation without active intervention.</p></p>
