<p><p>The 3D print surgical guide market comprises two primary material types: ceramic and resin. Ceramic materials are known for their biocompatibility and durability, making them ideal for long-lasting surgical applications. They provide excellent stability and can mimic the mechanical properties of bone, enhancing precision during procedures. In contrast, resin materials are favored for their versatility and ability to produce intricate designs with high detail. They are lightweight and suitable for customizing guides for various surgical specializations, enhancing surgical accuracy.</p></p>
