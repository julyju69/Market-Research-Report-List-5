<p><p>The commercial food dehydrator market offers two main types: stackable food dehydrators and shelf dehydrators. Stackable dehydrators feature multiple trays that can be vertically stacked, making them space-efficient and ideal for smaller operations. They allow for flexible drying of various foods simultaneously. Shelf dehydrators, on the other hand, consist of fixed shelves that provide a larger drying surface area, suitable for high-capacity needs. These units are often used in larger operations like restaurants or food production facilities to process bulk quantities efficiently.</p></p>
