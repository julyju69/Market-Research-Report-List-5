<p><p>The pneumatic chuck market can be categorized into three main types: built-in, front-end, and tie rod types. Built-in chucks are integrated directly into machinery, providing compactness and ease of installation. Front-end chucks offer accessibility for quick setup and maintenance, often used in applications requiring quick tool changes. Tie rod type chucks utilize external rods for clamping, allowing stable gripping and high clamping forces. Each type serves specific industrial needs, enhancing operational efficiency and precision in machining processes.</p></p>
