<p><ul><li>Closed-loop Sensors</li><li>Open-loop Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial current sensor market features two primary types: closed-loop and open-loop sensors. Closed-loop sensors, often utilizing magnetic components, provide high accuracy by measuring feedback on the current flowing through a conductor. They are ideal for applications requiring precision. In contrast, open-loop sensors operate based on direct measurement without feedback, offering simpler designs and lower costs, making them suitable for general-purpose applications. Both types cater to varying needs in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
