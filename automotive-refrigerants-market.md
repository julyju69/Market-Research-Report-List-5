<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Refrigerants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>R134a</li><li>R1234yf</li><li>R12</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive refrigerants market includes types like R134a, R1234yf, R12, and others, each serving as cooling agents in vehicle air conditioning systems. R134a, a hydrofluorocarbon (HFC), is widely used but has high global warming potential (GWP). R1234yf, a hydrofluoroolefin (HFO), is eco-friendly with low GWP, increasingly replacing R134a. R12, a chlorofluorocarbon (CFC), is phased out due to ozone depletion. Other refrigerants include blends and alternatives, focusing on sustainability and regulatory compliance. The market is driven by environmental concerns, regulations, and advancements in refrigerant technology.</p></p>
