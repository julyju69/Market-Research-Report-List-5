<p><strong>The Portable Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A- Scan</li><li>B- Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymeter</li><li>Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems market encompasses various types of diagnostic tools. A-Scan provides axial measurements of the eye, primarily used for biometry. B-Scan offers two-dimensional images, helpful for assessing retinal conditions. Combined Scan integrates both A- and B-Scan functionalities for comprehensive analysis. Pachymeters specifically measure corneal thickness, crucial for glaucoma evaluation. Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM) delivers high-resolution images of the anterior segment, aiding in the diagnosis of various ocular disorders. Each type plays a vital role in modern ophthalmic diagnostics.</p></p>
