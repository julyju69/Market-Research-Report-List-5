<p><p>Laboratory nitrogen evaporators are essential tools for sample concentration and solvent removal in various scientific applications. The market includes two primary types: metal dry baths and water baths. Metal dry baths utilize controlled heating elements to provide rapid, uniform temperature distribution, ensuring efficient evaporation. In contrast, water baths offer gentle heating through water immersion, ideal for sensitive samples that require gradual temperature increases. Both systems are designed to optimize evaporation rates while maintaining sample integrity and enhancing laboratory productivity.</p></p>
