<p><ul><li>Perforated Manual Film</li><li>Perforated Machine Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ventilated stretch film market comprises two main types: perforated manual film and perforated machine film. Perforated manual film is designed for hand applications, featuring small holes that allow air circulation, preventing moisture buildup during storage and transporting goods. In contrast, perforated machine film is used with automated packaging systems and offers higher efficiency in wrapping products while maintaining ventilation. Both types enhance product preservation and reduce spoilage, making them essential for industries requiring effective packaging solutions.</p></p>
