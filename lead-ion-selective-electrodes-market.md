In contrast, PVC Membrane electrodes incorporate a plasticized polyvinyl chloride matrix, providing flexibility and ease of manufacture while delivering satisfactory performance. Both types cater to diverse analytical needs in sectors such as water quality monitoring and chemical analysis.</p></p>
