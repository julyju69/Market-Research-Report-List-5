Horizontal units are designed for space efficiency and are typically placed in basements or mechanical rooms. Vertical units maximize vertical space, making them suitable for buildings with limited floor area. Ceiling-mounted units are installed above ceilings, saving floor space and providing discreet air distribution, ideal for office buildings and commercial spaces where aesthetics and space management are crucial.</p></p>
