<p><p>The Continuous Blood Glucose Monitoring market is primarily divided into two types: Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose Systems (SMBG) and Continuous Glucose Monitoring Systems (CGMS). SMBG involves traditional methods where patients use handheld devices to measure blood glucose levels at specific intervals, providing snapshots of their glucose status. In contrast, CGMS offers real-time monitoring, utilizing sensors placed under the skin to continuously track glucose levels throughout the day, enabling users to make timely decisions regarding their diabetes management.</p></p>
