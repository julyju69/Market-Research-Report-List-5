<p><ul><li>Single-sided</li><li>Double-sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV tape for special applications encompasses both single-sided and double-sided tape types, each serving distinct purposes. Single-sided UV tape features adhesive on one side, ideal for securing items or masking surfaces during UV processing. In contrast, double-sided UV tape has adhesive on both sides, facilitating the bonding of two surfaces effectively, making it suitable for applications requiring a strong, invisible bond. Both types are designed to withstand UV light exposure, ensuring durability and performance in specialized environments.</p></p>
