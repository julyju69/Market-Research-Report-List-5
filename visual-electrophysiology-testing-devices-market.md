<p><strong>The Visual Electrophysiology Testing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multifocal Electroretinogram</li><li>Visual-Evoked Responses</li><li>Electroretinogram</li><li>Electro-Oculogram</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Visual electrophysiology testing devices assess the eye's functional responses through various methods. The multifocal electroretinogram (mfERG) evaluates local retinal activity for conditions like macular degeneration. Visual-evoked responses (VER) measure the brain's electrical activity in response to visual stimuli, aiding in diagnosing optic nerve issues. The electroretinogram (ERG) captures the overall retinal response to light, essential for identifying retinal disorders. Lastly, the electro-oculogram (EOG) monitors eye movement and position, providing insights into disorders affecting vision or eye muscles.</p></p>
