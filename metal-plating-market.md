<p><strong>The Metal Plating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electroplating</li><li>Electroless Plating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal plating market comprises two primary types: electroplating and electroless plating. Electroplating involves using an electric current to deposit a metal layer onto a substrate, enhancing corrosion resistance and aesthetic appeal. In contrast, electroless plating relies on chemical reduction to coat surfaces without the need for electricity, allowing for uniform deposits on complex geometries. Both methods are vital for industries like automotive, electronics, and aerospace, providing improved durability, conductivity, and surface finish to various components.</p></p>
