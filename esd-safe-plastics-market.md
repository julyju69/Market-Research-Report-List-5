<p><ul><li>Ionic Type</li><li>Non-ionic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ESD-safe plastics market is categorized into two main types: ionic and non-ionic. Ionic ESD-safe plastics incorporate materials that generate static discharge through ionization, effectively neutralizing electrostatic charges. Meanwhile, non-ionic ESD-safe plastics use conductive additives or coatings that dissipate static without relying on ions. Both types serve crucial roles in protecting sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge, with applications ranging from packaging to manufacturing in industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
