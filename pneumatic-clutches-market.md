<p><strong>The Pneumatic Clutches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Friction Type</li><li>Toothed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic clutches market primarily features two types: friction type and toothed type. Friction type clutches use friction materials to engage and disengage power transmission, offering smooth operation and precise control, making them suitable for applications requiring gradual start and stop. Toothed type clutches, on the other hand, rely on interlocking teeth to achieve engagement, providing higher torque transmission and rapid response. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive and manufacturing, enhancing machinery efficiency and performance.</p></p>
