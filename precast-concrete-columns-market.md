<p><p>The precast concrete columns market is categorized into frame and non-frame columns. Frame columns, commonly used in structural systems, provide support for beams and slabs, creating a framework for buildings. They offer high strength and versatility, making them ideal for high-rise constructions. Non-frame columns, on the other hand, are typically used as standalone supports, often in applications like bridges or parking structures, where load-bearing requirements differ. Both types contribute to efficient construction and architectural design.</p></p>
