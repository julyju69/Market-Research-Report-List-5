<p><p>The semiconductor etching systems market is divided into two primary types: dry etching and wet etching systems. Dry etching uses gaseous chemicals to remove material, offering precise control and high resolution, crucial for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, wet etching involves liquid chemicals to dissolve materials, typically being faster and less expensive but with less precision. Both methods play vital roles in the fabrication of semiconductor devices, catering to various applications and technological requirements within the industry.</p></p>
