<p><strong>The Wood Particle Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood particle board is categorized into two main types based on their adhesive content and performance characteristics. Type I particle board is manufactured with moisture-resistant adhesives, making it suitable for environments with higher humidity levels, such as kitchens and bathrooms. In contrast, Type II particle board uses standard adhesives, making it ideal for dry environments like furniture and cabinetry. The distinction between these types is crucial for applications where durability and moisture resistance are important factors in material selection.</p></p>
