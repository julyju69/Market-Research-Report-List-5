<p><p>Pastry margarine is a specialized fat used in the baking industry, designed to enhance the texture and flavor of pastries, croissants, and other baked goods. Additionally, the rise of artisanal and gourmet baking trends is boosting demand for high-quality pastry margarine, further propelling market growth.
