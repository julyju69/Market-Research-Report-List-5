<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point of Entry (POE)</li><li>Point of Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential water treatment market comprises two primary types: Point of Entry (POE) and Point of Use (POU) systems. POE systems treat water at the entry point to a home, ensuring the entire household receives filtered water, effectively addressing issues like hardness and contaminants. In contrast, POU systems focus on treating water at specific outlets, such as faucets or showerheads, delivering filtered water for drinking or cooking. Both types cater to different needs, improving water quality for residential use.</p></p>
