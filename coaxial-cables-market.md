<p><p>The coaxial cable market is primarily divided into two types: baseband coaxial cables and broadband coaxial cables. Baseband coaxial cable is designed for digital signals, primarily used in local area networks (LANs) and short-distance communications, enabling high-speed data transfer. In contrast, broadband coaxial cable is optimized for carrying multiple signals over longer distances, commonly used in cable television and internet services, providing higher bandwidth to support video, voice, and data transmission simultaneously.</p></p>
