<p><strong>The Label-Free Detection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Plasmon Resonance</li><li>Bio-layer Interferometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Label-Free Detection market consists of Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR) and Bio-layer Interferometry technologies. SPR technology detects changes in refractive index near a sensor surface as biomolecules bind, providing real-time data on binding kinetics and affinity. Bio-layer Interferometry measures biomolecular interactions based on interference patterns caused by changes in optical thickness on a biosensor tip. Both technologies are widely used in pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries for drug discovery, protein-protein interactions, and biomarker research due to their ability to analyze biomolecular interactions without the need for labeling.</p></p>
