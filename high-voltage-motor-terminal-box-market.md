<p><ul><li>Traditional</li><li>PSTB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Voltage Motor Terminal Box market encompasses two main types: Traditional and PSTB (Phase Shifted Transformer Box). Traditional terminal boxes are standard enclosures designed for connecting motor cables, providing basic protection and functionality. In contrast, PSTB units are advanced configurations that incorporate phase-shifting technology, enhancing efficiency and reducing energy losses in multi-phase motor systems. Both types cater to specific industrial applications, with PSTB offering improved performance in demanding operational environments compared to traditional solutions.</p></p>
