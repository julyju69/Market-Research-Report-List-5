<p><p>The patient portal market comprises two main types: standalone patient portals and integrated patient portals. Standalone patient portals function independently, providing patients access to their health information, appointment scheduling, and communication with providers without being linked to larger health systems. In contrast, integrated patient portals are part of broader electronic health record (EHR) systems, offering seamless connectivity to a variety of health services and data, allowing for a more comprehensive patient experience and streamlined healthcare management.</p></p>
