<p><strong>The Radial Split Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage pump</li><li>Two-stage pump</li><li>Multi-stage pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radial split pump market consists of three primary types based on the number of stages: single-stage, two-stage, and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps feature one impeller and are ideal for low-pressure applications. Two-stage pumps incorporate two impellers, allowing for higher pressure and flow rates suitable for intermediate applications. Multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers, enabling the handling of high pressure requirements, making them suitable for demanding industrial processes. Each type serves distinct operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
