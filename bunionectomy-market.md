<p><strong>The Bunionectomy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Osteotomy</li><li>Exostectomy</li><li>Arthrodesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bunionectomy market encompasses various surgical techniques for treating bunions. Osteotomy involves cutting and realigning the bone to correct the deformity. Exostectomy focuses on removing the bony prominence at the base of the big toe without realigning the joint, often used for mild cases. Arthrodesis, or joint fusion, involves fusing the affected joint to alleviate pain and stabilize the foot in severe bunion cases. Each approach targets different severity levels and patient needs, influencing surgical outcomes and recovery.</p></p>
