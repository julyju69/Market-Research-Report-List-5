<p><strong>The Fiberglass Recycling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grinding</li><li>Incineration</li><li>Pyrolysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass recycling market involves various methods for processing waste materials. Grinding is a mechanical method that reduces fiberglass into smaller particles for reuse in composite materials. Incineration involves burning fiberglass waste, converting it into energy, though it may release harmful emissions. Pyrolysis utilizes high temperatures in an oxygen-free environment to break down fiberglass into oils, gases, and char, allowing for valuable material recovery. Each method has unique environmental implications and potential for resource recovery in fiberglass recycling.</p></p>
