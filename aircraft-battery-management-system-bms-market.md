<p><p>The Aircraft Battery Management System (BMS) is crucial for monitoring and managing battery health, ensuring safety and performance. The market comprises three types: </p><p>1. **Centralized BMS** consolidates control into one unit, simplifying management but can become a single point of failure.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Modular BMS** divides functionality into separate modules, enhancing flexibility and scalability for various aircraft sizes.</p><p>3. **Distributed BMS** involves multiple control units working collectively, improving redundancy and reliability, ideal for complex electric systems in advanced aircraft designs.</p></p>
