<p><p>The decorative film market comprises several types, each serving distinct purposes. Solar control window film reduces heat and glare, enhancing energy efficiency in buildings. Safety/security window film bolsters glass resistance to impact, preventing shattering and ensuring occupant safety. General glass film adds aesthetic value and privacy without sacrificing light. Spectrally selective window film filters specific wavelengths of light, allowing natural illumination while blocking harmful UV rays and excess heat, contributing to improved indoor comfort and energy savings.</p></p>
