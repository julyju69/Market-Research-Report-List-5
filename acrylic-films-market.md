<p><ul><li>Soft Type</li><li>Hard Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic films market is divided into soft type and hard type films, each catering to specific applications. Soft type acrylic films are flexible, often used in packaging, signage, and protective applications due to their durability and clarity. In contrast, hard type acrylic films are more rigid, providing superior impact resistance and scratch protection, making them suitable for applications like automotive parts and high-performance displays. Both types are valued for their optical clarity, weather resistance, and lightweight properties.</p></p>
