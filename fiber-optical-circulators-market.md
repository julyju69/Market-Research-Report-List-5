<p><p>Fiber optical circulators are devices that direct light in a specific direction through optical fibers, improving signal routing in communication networks. The market is segmented into 3-port and 4-port circulators. </p><p>3-port circulators manage light flow among three connections, ideal for simple applications like fiber optic sensors and amplifiers. In contrast, 4-port circulators enable more complex routing among four connections, suitable for advanced systems like coherent communication and multiplexing, enhancing versatility and functionality in optical networks.</p></p>
