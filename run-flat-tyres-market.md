<p><strong>The Run-Flat Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-supporting</li><li>Self-sealing</li><li>Auxiliary-supported</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The run-flat tyre market consists of three main types: self-supporting, self-sealing, and auxiliary-supported tyres. Self-supporting tyres maintain their shape and support vehicle weight even after a loss of air, allowing for limited driving distance. Self-sealing tyres feature a special inner lining that automatically seals punctures, preventing air loss. Auxiliary-supported tyres are equipped with an additional support ring or structure that provides stability when deflated, ensuring continued mobility. Each type enhances safety and convenience during tyre failure incidents.</p></p>
