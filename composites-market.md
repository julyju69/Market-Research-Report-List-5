<p><p>The composites market encompasses various manufacturing techniques, each suited for different applications. Layup involves manually placing layers of composite materials, ideal for custom shapes. Filament winding creates strong cylindrical components by winding resin-coated fibers around a mandrel. Injection molding combines composite materials into a mold for high-volume production of complex shapes. Pultrusion continuously pulls reinforced materials through a heated die, resulting in constant cross-sectional profiles. Other methods include resin transfer molding and vacuum-assisted resin infusion, catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
