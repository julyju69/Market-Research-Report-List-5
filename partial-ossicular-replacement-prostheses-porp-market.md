<p><p>Partial Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (PORP) are medical devices used in ear surgeries to reconstruct the ossicular chain in cases of conductive hearing loss. The market for PORPs is experiencing significant growth due to factors such as increasing incidences of hearing loss, advancements in surgical techniques, and rising awareness about otological diseases.</p><p>The demand for more effective and biocompatible materials is driving innovation in the development of PORPs.
