<p><p>Automotive ventilated seats are available in two main types: suction type and air supply type. Suction type ventilated seats utilize a suction system to draw out hot air and moisture from the seat, keeping the driver and passengers cool and comfortable. On the other hand, air supply type ventilated seats blow cool air through the seat, providing a constant flow of fresh air to reduce sweating and enhance overall comfort. Both types offer a convenient and effective way to maintain a comfortable driving experience.</p></p>
