<p><p>The CAT scanner market encompasses various advanced imaging technologies, notably C-Arm and O-Arm systems. C-Arm devices provide real-time fluoroscopic imaging, commonly used in surgical settings for guidance during procedures. In contrast, O-Arm systems offer multi-dimensional imaging capabilities, integrating both 2D and 3D imaging for enhanced visualization. These systems are pivotal in orthopedic, neurosurgery, and trauma cases, optimizing surgical outcomes by enabling precise and instantaneous imaging. Both technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy and improve patient care in clinical environments.</p></p>
