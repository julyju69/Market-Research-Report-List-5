<p><p>The ink ingredients market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Solvent-based inks** use organic solvents that evaporate, providing vibrant colors and quick drying times, ideal for various surfaces.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Water-based inks** use water as a carrier, offering eco-friendly options suitable for packaging and printing on porous substrates.</p><p>   </p><p>3. **Oil-based inks** utilize oils for a glossy finish, commonly employed in high-quality prints and packaging.</p><p>   </p><p>4. **UV-based inks** cure with ultraviolet light, providing durability and vibrant colors, often used in high-speed printing applications.</p></p>
