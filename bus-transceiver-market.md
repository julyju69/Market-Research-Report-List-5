<p><p>The bus transceiver market encompasses three primary technologies: BiCMOS, bipolar, and CMOS. BiCMOS combines the advantages of bipolar and CMOS technologies, offering high speed and low power consumption, making it suitable for complex applications. Bipolar transceivers provide excellent performance in terms of speed and drive capability but consume more power. CMOS transceivers are known for their low power consumption and high integration capabilities, making them ideal for battery-operated devices. Each type addresses specific requirements in digital communication systems.</p></p>
