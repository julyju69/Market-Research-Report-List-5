<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Couplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Couplers</li><li>Multi-mode Couplers</li><li>Polarization Maintaining (PM) Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic couplers market consists of three primary types: single mode couplers, multi-mode couplers, and polarization maintaining (PM) couplers. Single mode couplers are designed for long-distance communication, efficiently transmitting light through a single strand of fiber. Multi-mode couplers support multiple light paths, ideal for shorter distances. Polarization maintaining couplers preserve the light's polarization state, essential for specific applications in telecommunications and sensing. Each type caters to different needs in terms of performance, distance, and application suitability.</p></p>
