CCD-based imaging colorimeters utilize charge-coupled device technology for high sensitivity and image quality, making them ideal for precise color measurements in professional settings. Conversely, CMOS-based devices offer faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and integration flexibility, making them suitable for consumer applications and portable devices. Each technology serves distinct market needs.</p></p>
