<p><p>The spray guns market is mainly categorized into two types: ordinary and pressurized spray guns. Ordinary spray guns operate on gravity or suction, using air pressure to atomize paint, making them suitable for smaller tasks and DIY projects. In contrast, pressurized spray guns utilize a pressurized tank to deliver a consistent spray and are ideal for larger-scale applications, offering greater efficiency and a smooth finish. Each type caters to different user needs, influencing their adoption in various industries.</p></p>
