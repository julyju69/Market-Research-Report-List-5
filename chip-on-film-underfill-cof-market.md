<p><ul><li>Capillary Underfill (CUF)</li><li>No Flow Underfill (NUF)</li><li>Non-Conductive Paste (NCP) Underfill</li><li>Non-Conductive Film (NCF) Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill (MUF) Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip On Film Underfill (COF) refers to the materials used to enhance the mechanical and thermal reliability of semiconductor devices. The market comprises various underfill types: </p><p>1. Capillary Underfill (CUF) utilizes capillary action to fill gaps.</p><p>2. No Flow Underfill (NUF) remains in place, preventing movement.</p><p>3. Non-Conductive Paste (NCP) offers an adhesive solution without conductivity.</p><p>4. Non-Conductive Film (NCF) provides a solid film that unobtrusively supports components.</p><p>5. Molded Underfill (MUF) involves polymer molding for added structural integrity.</p></p>
