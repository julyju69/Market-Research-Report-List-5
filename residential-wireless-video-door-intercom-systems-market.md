<p><p>Residential wireless video door intercom systems primarily include two market types: Wi-Fi type and radio frequency (RF) type. Wi-Fi systems leverage existing home networks, enabling high-quality video streaming and integration with smart home devices. They offer flexibility but depend on stable internet connectivity. RF systems operate independently of Wi-Fi, using specific frequencies for communication, ensuring reliability in areas with weak internet signals but often with lower video quality. Both types cater to security needs, enhancing home monitoring and communication.</p></p>
