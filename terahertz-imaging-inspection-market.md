<p><strong>The Terahertz Imaging Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Inspection Market is divided into two main types: passive and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging relies on detecting natural radiation emitted by objects, making it suitable for non-invasive inspections without requiring an external energy source. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves the use of external terahertz radiation sources to illuminate the target, enhancing the detection of materials and structures. Both methods are essential for applications in security, materials characterization, and industrial quality control.</p></p>
