<p><p>The Smart Card OEM/ODM market consists of solutions integrating One-Time Password (OTP) and Multifactor Authentication (MFA) technologies. OTP provides a dynamic, time-sensitive password for secure transactions, reducing the risk of unauthorized access. On the other hand, MFA enhances security by requiring multiple forms of verification, such as something the user knows (password), something they have (smart card), or something they are (biometrics). Both methods are essential in bolstering security across various industries, including finance, healthcare, and government.</p></p>
