<p><p>The ORP sensor market comprises three primary types: glass type sensors, ISFET sensors, and others. Glass type sensors utilize a specialized glass electrode to measure oxidation-reduction potential, offering high accuracy and stability. ISFET sensors employ ion-sensitive field-effect transistor technology for a compact, rugged design suitable for harsh environments. Other types may include membrane or combination sensors, providing various alternatives for specific applications, enhancing versatility in monitoring water quality, industrial processes, and environmental assessments.</p></p>
