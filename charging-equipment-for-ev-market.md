<p><p>The EV charging equipment market is categorized into Level 2 and Level 3 charging solutions. In contrast, Level 3 chargers, or DC fast chargers, operate at high voltages, enabling rapid charging in under an hour, making them ideal for highway locations and commercial applications, facilitating long-distance travel and reducing downtime for EV users.</p></p>
