<p><p>The Pipe-Tank Coatings market is primarily divided into two types: powder and liquid coatings. Powder coatings are applied electrostatically and cured under heat, resulting in a durable, uniform finish that is resistant to chipping and fading. Liquid coatings, on the other hand, are applied using conventional methods such as brushing or spraying, offering versatility and ease of application. Both types serve to protect pipes and tanks from corrosion, chemical exposure, and environmental factors, ensuring longevity and performance.</p></p>
