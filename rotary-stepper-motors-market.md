<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet (PM)</li><li>Variable Relutance (VR)</li><li>Hybrid (HB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary stepper motors market is categorized into three types: Permanent Magnet (PM), Variable Reluctance (VR), and Hybrid (HB). PM stepper motors utilize permanent magnets for rotor magnetization, offering high torque and efficiency. VR stepper motors are designed with a toothed rotor that aligns with stator magnetic fields, providing precise rotation and lower cost but less torque. Hybrid stepper motors combine features of both PM and VR, delivering improved accuracy and performance, making them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
