<p><p>The plastic and composite dispersants market is categorized into organic and inorganic types. Organic dispersants, derived from natural or synthetic polymers, enhance the distribution of particles within plastic matrices, improving stability and performance. In contrast, inorganic dispersants consist of mineral-based compounds that also promote particle dispersion but may offer superior thermal stability. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing material properties, enhancing processing efficiency, and ensuring consistency in various applications across industries such as automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
