<p><p>The surfboard skeg market features two primary types: glass-on skegs and removable skeg systems. Conversely, removable skeg systems allow for customization and ease of transport, enabling surfers to switch skegs based on conditions or preferences. Both types cater to different surfing styles and levels, with glass-on skegs often favored for performance, while removable systems offer versatility and convenience.</p></p>
