<p><p>The cancer ablation devices market encompasses various technologies used to destroy cancerous tissue. Cryoablation uses extreme cold, while High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) employs focused sound waves. Laser Interstitial Thermal Therapy (LiTT) utilizes laser energy for targeted heating. Microwave Ablation (MWA) generates heat through microwave energy, and Radiofrequency Ablation (RFA) uses radio waves to induce heat. Irreversible Electroporation (IRE) disrupts cell membranes using electrical pulses, whereas Non-Thermal Ablation techniques damage cells without significant heat, offering diverse treatment options for patients.</p></p>
