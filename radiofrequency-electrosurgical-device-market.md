<p><ul><li>Monopolar Devices</li><li>Bipolar Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radiofrequency Electrosurgical Device market comprises two primary types: monopolar and bipolar devices. Monopolar devices utilize a single active electrode to deliver RF energy, requiring a grounding pad to complete the circuit, making them versatile for various surgical applications. In contrast, bipolar devices have two electrodes that are placed close together, allowing for localized energy delivery, which minimizes damage to surrounding tissues. Both types offer advantages in precision, efficiency, and control during surgical procedures, contributing to their growing adoption in healthcare.</p></p>
