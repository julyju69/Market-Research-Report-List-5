<p><p>Veterinary imaging equipment encompasses various technologies used for diagnostic purposes in animals. Radiography (X-Ray) systems provide quick imaging of bones and tissues, while ultrasound imaging systems utilize sound waves for soft tissue evaluation. Computed tomography (CT) offers detailed cross-sectional images, and video endoscopy allows for direct visualization of internal organs. Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) provides high-resolution images of soft tissues. Other devices may include thermography and nuclear imaging, contributing to a comprehensive diagnostic toolkit for veterinary care.</p></p>
