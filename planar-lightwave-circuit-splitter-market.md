<p><strong>The Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1N</li><li>2N</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) splitter market is segmented primarily into 1N and 2N types. A 1N splitter distributes an optical signal from one input to N outputs, making it ideal for applications requiring multiple connections from a single source. In contrast, a 2N splitter divides the optical signal from two inputs into N outputs, providing more flexibility in network design. Both types are crucial in fiber optic networks, enhancing signal distribution efficiency in telecommunications and broadband applications.</p></p>
