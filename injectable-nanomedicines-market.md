<p><p>The injectable nanomedicines market comprises various types including liposomes, micelles, nanocrystals, and polymeric nanoparticles. Liposomes are spherical vesicles that encapsulate drugs, facilitating targeted delivery. Micelles form from surfactants and improve solubility for poorly water-soluble medications. Nanocrystals enhance the bioavailability of drugs by reducing particle size. Polymeric nanoparticles are engineered for controlled release and targeting. Together with other emerging formulations, these nanomedicines enhance therapeutic efficacy and minimize side effects in various medical applications.</p></p>
