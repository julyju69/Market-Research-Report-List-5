<p><strong>The Rubber Filling Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Naphthenic Base</li><li>Paraffin Base</li><li>Aromatic Base</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber filling oil market consists of three main types: naphthenic base, paraffin base, and aromatic base oils. Naphthenic base oils are valued for their low volatility and excellent lubricity, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Paraffin base oils are known for their stability and high viscosity index, providing improved durability. Aromatic base oils offer superior processing characteristics and compatibility with rubber, enhancing the overall performance of rubber products. Each type serves specific needs in the rubber compounding industry.</p></p>
