<p><p>The flexible plastic packaging market is categorized primarily into four types: Polypropylene (PP), Polyethylene (PE), Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET), and others. PP offers high clarity, moisture resistance, and is widely used for food packaging. PE is known for its flexibility and durability, making it suitable for bags and wraps. PET is valued for its strength and barrier properties, often used in bottles and trays. The "others" category includes materials like polystyrene and biodegradable plastics, catering to niche applications.</p></p>
