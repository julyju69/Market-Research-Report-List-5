<p><p>The Ultraviolet Phototherapy Instrument market is divided into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I instruments are primarily used for treating skin conditions such as psoriasis and eczema with targeted UVB therapy, delivering effective results through narrowband technology. In contrast, Type II instruments often utilize PUVA therapy, combining UVA radiation with a photosensitizing agent for more severe skin disorders. Both types aim to reduce inflammation and promote healing, but they cater to different treatment protocols and patient needs.</p></p>
