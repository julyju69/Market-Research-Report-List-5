<p><ul><li>Unshaped (monolithic refractories)</li><li>Shaped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fire Brick Market comprises two main types: Unshaped (monolithic refractories) and Shaped. Unshaped refractories are flexible, non-preformed materials like castables, mortars, and gunning mixes, used for lining and repairs in high-temperature applications. They offer adaptability and ease of installation. Shaped refractories are pre-formed bricks or blocks designed for specific applications, providing structural stability and durability in furnaces, kilns, and reactors. Both types cater to industries like steel, cement, and glass, ensuring thermal insulation and resistance to extreme heat and corrosion.</p></p>
