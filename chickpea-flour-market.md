<p><strong>The Chickpea Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Desi</li><li>Kabuli</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chickpea flour is primarily classified into two market types: Desi and Kabuli. Desi chickpeas are smaller, darker, and more robust, often used in traditional dishes across South Asia, and their flour is known for a stronger flavor and higher nutritional value. Kabuli chickpeas, larger and lighter in color, are more popular in Western cuisines and produce a milder flour. Both types cater to distinct culinary preferences and markets, influencing their pricing, demand, and applications in various recipes.</p></p>
