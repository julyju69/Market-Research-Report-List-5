<p><p>The eyelid tape market consists of two main types: single-sided tape and double-sided tape. Single-sided tape adheres to the eyelid's surface, providing a subtle lift and enhancing the appearance of monolid eyes without altering natural features. In contrast, double-sided tape is applied between the eyelid layers, creating a more pronounced fold for a dramatic look. Both types cater to varying preferences for eyelid aesthetics, with consumers choosing based on desired effects, ease of application, and comfort.</p></p>
