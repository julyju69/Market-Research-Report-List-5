<p><p>The coated battery separator market can be categorized into two main types: wet process and dry process. The wet process involves applying a coating solution to porous separator materials, enhancing their performance and ion conductivity. This method typically results in improved mechanical properties and thermal stability. Conversely, the dry process entails a coating application without solvents, often resulting in faster production times and reduced environmental impact. Both processes aim to optimize battery efficiency and lifespan across various applications.</p></p>
