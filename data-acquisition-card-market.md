PXI cards are designed for modular instrumentation systems, offering high-speed data transfer and synchronization. CPCI cards are robust and suitable for industrial applications, supporting standardized interoperability. PCI cards, typically used in desktop computers, provide easy integration and cost-effectiveness for data acquisition tasks. Each type serves specific needs in industries like telecommunications, automation, and research, ensuring efficient data collection and processing across diverse applications.</p></p>
