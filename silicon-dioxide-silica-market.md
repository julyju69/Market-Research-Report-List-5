<p><p>The silica market includes three main types: pyrogenic silica, precipitated silica, and natural silica. Pyrogenic silica, produced through the combustion of silicon compounds, is known for its high purity and is used in applications such as cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. Precipitated silica is chemically synthesized and widely used in tires, rubber, and food products due to its thickening and anti-caking properties. Natural silica, extracted from quartz, is used in glass, ceramics, and construction materials, offering a cost-effective solution for various industrial needs.</p></p>
