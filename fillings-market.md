<p><strong>The Fillings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bakeable</li><li>No Bakeable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fillings markets can be categorized into bakeable and no-bakeable types. Bakeable fillings are designed to withstand heat during the baking process, making them ideal for baked goods like pies, pastries, and cookies. They often include fruit purees, jams, and creams that set well when cooked. In contrast, no-bakeable fillings are meant for chilled desserts or products that do not require baking, such as mousses, custards, and certain cream fillings. These offer convenience and quick preparation, appealing to a different consumer segment.</p></p>
