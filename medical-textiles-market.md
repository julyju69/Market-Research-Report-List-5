<p><strong>The Medical Textiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-woven Fabric</li><li>Woven Fabric</li><li>Knitted Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical textiles market is categorized into non-woven, woven, and knitted fabrics. Non-woven fabrics are created by bonding fibers through various processes, offering excellent fluid resistance and breathability, making them ideal for disposable products like masks and gowns. Woven fabrics consist of interlaced yarns, providing strength and durability, commonly used in surgical garments and drapes. Knitted fabrics, made by interlocking loops of yarn, offer elasticity and comfort, suitable for bandages and orthopedic supports. Each type serves unique applications in healthcare.</p></p>
