<p><p>The Smart Range Hood market features two primary types: Side Suction and Direct Suction. Side Suction hoods are designed to draw in cooking fumes from the sides, providing efficient ventilation while minimizing noise, suitable for various kitchen layouts. Direct Suction hoods, on the other hand, capture smoke and odors directly above the cooking surface, ensuring optimal air quality. Both types integrate smart technology for enhanced control, allowing users to adjust settings remotely and monitor performance for a cleaner cooking environment.</p></p>
