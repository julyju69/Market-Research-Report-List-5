<p><p>The image printing sensor market encompasses various printing technologies, including inkjet, flexo, and screen printing. Inkjet printing utilizes tiny nozzles to spray ink droplets onto surfaces, offering high-resolution and versatility for various materials. Flexographic printing employs flexible relief plates and is ideal for high-volume, continuous print runs, especially in packaging. Screen printing utilizes a mesh to transfer ink, producing vibrant colors and durability, making it suitable for textiles and promotional products. Each method serves distinct applications and industries, driving market growth.</p></p>
