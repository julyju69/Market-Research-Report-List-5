<p><strong>The Active-pixel Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buried Photodiode-structure</li><li>Selection Transistor</li><li>MOS Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active-Pixel Sensor (APS) market is categorized into three key types: Buried Photodiode-structure, Selection Transistor, and MOS Amplifier. Buried Photodiode-structure enhances light sensitivity by placing the photodiode beneath the surface, improving signal quality. The Selection Transistor acts as an electronic switch, enabling individual pixel readouts for enhanced control and efficiency. MOS Amplifiers amplify the captured signals, ensuring high-quality image output. These types cater to various applications, including consumer electronics, medical imaging, and automotive systems, driving innovation in imaging technologies.</p></p>
