<p><p>The photonic crystal market is categorized into three main types: One-Dimensional (1D), Two-Dimensional (2D), and Three-Dimensional (3D) photonic crystals. 1D photonic crystals feature layered structures that control light propagation in one direction, primarily used in optical coatings. 2D photonic crystals manipulate light in a plane, finding applications in waveguides and sensors. 3D photonic crystals offer superior light control in all directions, leading to advancements in telecommunications and imaging technologies. Each type serves distinct applications and market needs.</p></p>
