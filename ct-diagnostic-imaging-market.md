<p><strong>The CT Diagnostic Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional CT scanners</li><li>Cone beam CT scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT diagnostic imaging market can be categorized into two main types: conventional CT scanners and cone beam CT (CBCT) scanners. Conventional CT scanners utilize rotating X-ray beams to produce cross-sectional images of the body, offering high-resolution data for a wide range of medical applications. In contrast, cone beam CT scanners are specifically designed for dental and maxillofacial imaging, providing 3D visualization with lower radiation doses. Both technologies are essential for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
