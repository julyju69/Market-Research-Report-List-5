<p><p>The corrugated paperboard market comprises several types, including single wall, double wall, and triple wall paperboard. Single wall paperboard consists of one layer of fluted material sandwiched between two liners, making it lightweight and cost-effective for basic packaging. Double wall board features two layers of fluted material for added strength and insulation, suitable for heavier items. Triple wall paperboard has three layers, offering maximum durability and protection for fragile cargo. Other types may include specialty boards designed for unique applications.</p></p>
