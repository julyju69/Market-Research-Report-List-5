<p><p>Denim fabric comes in various types based on its weight and thickness. Light denim fabric is typically less than 10 ounces per square yard and is used for making lighter garments like shirts and skirts. Medium denim fabric falls between 10-12 ounces per square yard and is commonly used for jeans and jackets. Heavy denim fabric is 12 ounces or more per square yard and is durable and sturdy, perfect for making heavy-duty garments like overalls and workwear. Each type caters to different styles and purposes in the market.</p></p>
