<p><p>The Glass Fiber Prepreg market is categorized primarily into two resin systems: epoxy and phenolic. Epoxy resin systems are renowned for their strong adhesion, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties, making them suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, phenolic resin systems are characterized by their excellent thermal stability and flame resistance, making them ideal for high-temperature environments and electrical insulation. Both types serve diverse industries, offering tailored solutions depending on performance requirements and application needs.</p></p>
