<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydro-cylinder market is primarily divided into two types: single-acting and double-acting cylinders. Single-acting cylinders utilize hydraulic pressure to extend the piston in one direction, relying on external forces for retraction, making them suitable for applications with a return spring. In contrast, double-acting cylinders can push and pull using hydraulic force in both directions, offering greater efficiency and control. These types cater to various industrial applications based on the required force and motion, enhancing operational productivity.</p></p>
