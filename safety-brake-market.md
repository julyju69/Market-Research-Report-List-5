<p><p>The safety brake market includes two primary types: insulated safety brakes and non-insulated safety brakes. Insulated safety brakes are designed with materials or coatings that prevent electrical conductivity, offering enhanced safety in environments with electric or high-voltage machinery. Non-insulated safety brakes, on the other hand, lack these insulating features, making them suitable for general mechanical applications where electrical risks are minimal. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring operational safety across various industries, including manufacturing and transportation.</p></p>
