<p><p>The meat stabilizer blends market is categorized into two main types: plant-based and animal-based blends. Plant-based meat stabilizers utilize natural ingredients like starches, proteins, and fibers derived from plants, catering to vegetarian and vegan alternatives. These blends enhance texture, moisture retention, and overall product quality. In contrast, animal-based meat stabilizers incorporate collagen, gelatin, and other meat-derived components, improving the mouthfeel and binding properties of traditional meat products. Both types aim to enhance food quality and consumer acceptance.</p></p>
