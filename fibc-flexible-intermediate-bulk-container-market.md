<p><p>FIBCs, or Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers, are used for transporting bulk materials. The market consists of four main types: </p><p>- Type A FIBCs are non-conductive and suitable for non-flammable materials.</p><p>- Type B FIBCs provide limited electrostatic discharge protection.</p><p>- Type C FIBCs are conductive and designed to prevent ignition hazards in flammable environments.</p><p>- Type D FIBCs are also conductive but can safely dissipate static charges without grounding. Each type addresses specific safety and handling requirements for various industries.</p></p>
