<p><p>Airline Reservation Systems (ARS) can be categorized into two main market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based ARS allows airlines to access their reservation systems over the internet, offering scalability, lower upfront costs, and ease of integration with other technologies. In contrast, On-Premises ARS requires airlines to install and maintain systems on their own servers, providing greater control and security but often involving higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance efforts. Each type serves different operational needs and budgets.</p></p>
