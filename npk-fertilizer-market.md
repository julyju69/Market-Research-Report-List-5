<p><p>The NPK fertilizer market features various types classified by their primary nutrient compositions. Chlorine-based compound fertilizers contain chloride as a key component, enhancing nutrient absorption but potentially harming sensitive crops. Sulfur-based fertilizers improve soil quality and nutrient uptake, especially in sulfur-deficient soils. Nitro-based fertilizers focus on nitrogen, crucial for plant growth, while urea-based fertilizers provide a concentrated nitrogen source, optimizing efficiency and reducing environmental impact. Each type addresses specific agricultural needs, promoting better crop yields and sustainable farming practices.</p></p>
